Guadalajara

Also known as "Guanatos" is MY favorite city to visit from the state of Jalisco. Primarily because that is where my family lives. There is humble home in "Lomas de Oblatos" where I like to call my second home. I basically grew up there. Every summer when class ended, I'd pack my bags and fly from LAX to GDL. There are so many beautiful landmarks. The only bad thing of Guadalajara is when vacation is over and you have to leave.

Tequila

You can't visit the state of Jalisco without swinging by Tequila. It goes without saying that you cannot visit Tequila without drinking some tequila drinks. Aside from the beautiful artistry, the only other souvenier is a tequila bottle or at least a hangover. Drink responsibly!

Tlaquepaque

While the entire state of Jalisco has many delicious spots for food and drinks, Tlaquepaque has so much to offer. There is a little street where you'll find several restaurants. In one of these restaurants is where I had my first "pepper shot", which is basically a shot of tequila served in a green pepper. It's an experience you can't miss out on. Spicy, but worth it!
